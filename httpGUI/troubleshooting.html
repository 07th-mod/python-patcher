Show Game and Install Logs

There are two types of logs. You should submit your game log (if you have one), and all your installer logs to our Discord in the Umineko #umi_support or Higurashi #higu_support channel when asking for help.

Installer logs

The installer logs are produced by this installer. They are titled `MOD-INSTALLER-LOG-[DATE].txt` This may be useful if your install didn't fully complete or seems stuck.

Game logs (for Higurashi)

The game log is produced when the game runs (for Higurashi). This is titled `output_log.txt`. This may be useful if your game freezes or crashes.

Open Save Folder (Windows)

In some cases, your game might be unable to boot because of one or more corrupted save files.
Since uninstalling the game doesn't clear your save files, you need to clear them manually.
If you are deleting your saves, please make a backup first.
